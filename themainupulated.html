Who’s in It & Their Roles

  • Ji Chang Wook plays Park Tae‑jung — an ordinary man whose life is shattered when he’s wrongfully accused of a heinous crime and sent to prison.
  • Doh Kyung Soo (also known as D.O. from the K-pop group EXO) stars as An Yo‑han, the mastermind behind the conspiracy — a cold, calculating antagonist who manipulates events to frame Tae-jung.
  • Other cast members include Lee Kwang Soo, Jo Yoon Soo, and Pyo Ye Jin, among others.

What’s the Story About

  • The gist: Tae-jung’s ordinary life gets destroyed when he’s falsely accused of a major crime — one he didn’t commit
  • Behind this tragedy is An Yo-han, who manipulates evidence and orchestrates a dark conspiracy that ruins Tae-jung’s life
  • Once Tae-jung realizes the truth, he embarks on a relentless revenge mission to expose the manipulation and clear his name
  • The drama promises intense action scenes, psychological tension, elaborate schemes, and a deep exploration of trust, betrayal, and justice
