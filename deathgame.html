Death game

Death Game (also known as Ijak-Game or Death’s Game) is a 2023–2024 Korean fantasy-thriller drama starring Seo In-guk and Park So-dam. It became very popular for its unique premise, emotional storytelling, and intense moral themes.

Main Characters

  • Choi Yi-jae — Seo In-guk A man crushed by society’s failures. His journey through 12 lives forces him to confront morality, empathy, and the consequences of his choices.
  • Death — Park So-dam Cold, intimidating, and vengeful. She forces Yi-jae to learn the value of life by putting him in the most painful and dangerous situations.
