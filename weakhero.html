Weak hero

Weak Hero Class 1 is a South Korean drama that aired in 2022.It’s based on a webtoon of the same name and is a unique mix of high school drama, action, and thriller. The show has gained attention for its distinct approach to the typical high school genre, focusing on violence, bullying, and the struggle of an underdog who isn’t exactly what he seems.

Main Characters:

  • Yeon Si-Eun (played by Park Ji-Hoon) – The protagonist, an unassuming high school student with a knack for using his intelligence and resourcefulness to deal with bullies and dangerous situations.
  • Ahn Su-Hyun (played by Choi Hyun-Wook) – A key character who becomes involved with Si-Eun as the story develops. He is a tough, rebellious student who is caught up in the violence of high school gangs.
  • Oh Beom-Seok (played by Hong Kyung) – Another important character who plays a part in Si-Eun’s journey. He is part of the gang violence in the school and one of the characters who adds to the story’s conflict.

Themes

  • Bullying and Violence – The show highlights the intense pressures and dangerous environments in high schools, especially when students resort to physical violence to assert dominance.
  • Underdog Story – Si-Eun is constantly underestimated because of his appearance, but he proves that strength isn’t just about physicality.
